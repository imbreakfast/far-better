Far Better

An attempt at improving upon the "Far Colder" HPI acronym using logic and pictures. A work-in-progress.

Have an idea? Email me at karl.santiago@tufts.edu — Thanks!

METHOD

Organize HPI data collection around 3 logical themes:

Space

Character "How would you describe the pain?"
Location "Where is the pain located?" or "Where does it start?"
Radiation "Does the pain travel elsewhere?"

Relation

Associated Symptoms "Are there any symptoms related to this pain?"
Relieving Factors "Does anything make the pain better?"
Exacerbating Factors "Does anything make the pain worse?"

Time

Onset "When did the pain start?" or "When does it start?"
Duration "How long does it last?"
Frequency "How often does it happen?"
